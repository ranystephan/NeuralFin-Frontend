## Features

The Dashboard Page offers many distinctive features and metrics that are tailored to your portfolio, and that are hardly found anywhere else.

1. Portfolio Creation and Formation
2. Performance assessment
3. Portfolio Risk Metrics
4. Portfolio Diversification Metrics


## What it consists of

Click on a section below to learn about the different metrics that the page offers.

<div className="grid gap-4 mt-6">

<Card href="/docs/dashboard/mv-pnl">

### Current Market Value and PnL

The current market value is the total value of your portfolio. The PnL is the profit or loss that you have made on your portfolio.

</Card>

<Card href="/docs/news/beta">

### Portfolio Beta

Beta is a measure of the volatility, or systematic risk, of a security or portfolio in comparison to the market as a whole.

</Card>

<Card href="/docs/dashboard/var">

### Value at Risk (VaR)

Value at Risk (VaR) is a statistic that measures and quantifies the level of financial risk within a firm, portfolio, or position over a specific time frame.

</Card>

<Card href="/docs/dashboard/es">

### Expected Shortfall (ES)

Expected Shortfall (ES) is a risk measure that evaluates the market risk or credit risk of a portfolio.

</Card>

<Card href="/docs/dashboard/asset-allocation">

### Asset Allocation Chart

The asset allocation chart shows the percentage of your portfolio that is invested in each asset class or industry.

</Card>

<Card href="/docs/dashboard/alpha">

### Portfolio Alpha

Alpha is a measure of the active return on an investment, the performance of that investment compared with a suitable market index.

</Card>
<Card href="/docs/dashboard/sharpe-ratio">

### Sharpe Ratio

The Sharpe ratio is a measure of risk-adjusted return, which compares an investment's excess return to its standard deviation of returns.

</Card>
<Card href="/docs/dashboard/information-ratio">

### Information Ratio (IR)

The information ratio (IR) is a measurement of portfolio returns beyond the returns of a benchmark, usually an index, compared to the volatility of those returns.

</Card>
<Card href="/docs/dashboard/information-coefficient">

### Information Coefficient (IC)

The information coefficient (IC) is a measure of the correlation between the predicted and actual values of a portfolio's returns.

</Card>
<Card href="/docs/dashboard/diversification-matrix">

### Diversification Matrix

The diversification matrix shows the correlation between each asset in your portfolio.

</Card>


</div>
